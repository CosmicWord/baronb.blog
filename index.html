Critical AI Bias Management

Prompt-Induced Bias

AI systems exhibit extreme sensitivity to evaluation prompts. Harsh analysis causes AI to force errors, while positive prompts lead to overlooking genuine issues.

Neutral Analysis Protocol

Solution involves neutral prompts requesting analysis of dimensional consistency, physical coherence, and mathematical validity without emotional language.

Multi-AI Contradiction Method

Essential to use multiple AI systems that may contradict each other – contradictions often reveal genuine issues requiring deeper investigation.

Note on Philosophy of Science Research

Philosophy of science research presents unique challenges with AI collaboration. AI systems often treat philosophical papers as empirical models, emphasizing predictive validation rather than conceptual contribution to philosophical debates. Human supervision is essential to ensure AI understands the actual purpose and scope of philosophical inquiry.

Ethics & Methodology

Personal Statement on AI Usage

I use artificial intelligence as a fundamental tool in my research, always under my own guidance and judgment. I see it strictly as a tool — not a replacement for human work. I take full responsibility for everything I produce and am fully aware of how I apply AI throughout the process.

All ideas, hypotheses, and theoretical frameworks in my papers are original and developed by me. AI is not a co-author. This can be verified through the structure and originality of the publications themselves.

Important Warning
